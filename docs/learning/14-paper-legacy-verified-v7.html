What did each workflow actually test, what went wrong before the audit, and what can the current negative result honestly mean?

The opening question

Can two honest $100 calculations answer different questions?

Suppose an index price begins at 100, ends at 100, and distributes a $2 dividend. A $100 investor owns the same market throughout the period. A price-only series records (100/100)−1=0%. A gross total-return series that reinvests the dividend records (100+2)/100−1=2%, so wealth becomes $102. Neither arithmetic operation is dishonest. They measure different return definitions.

Now feed years of those different returns into downside deviation, Sortino features, centroids, state paths, monthly validation, and a delayed backtest. A small input-definition difference can change the entire strategy. That is why the paper's licensed total-return series, the legacy Yahoo price workflow, and current free proxies cannot be treated as three implementations of one byte-identical experiment.

Correct comparison

Same question first

Compare outcomes only after checking data definition, date eligibility, fitting windows, online timing, selection, cash, cost, delay, metrics, and claim label.

A common failure: negative means refuted

Proxy mismatch ≠ paper false

A later free-source proxy that misses the paper's ordering is evidence about that proxy experiment. It cannot identify whether data, sample, implementation, or model behavior caused the difference.

The idea to rememberThe paper, legacy repository, and v7 run are three evidence objects with different inputs and protocols. Compare their contracts before their conclusions. The strongest honest current statement is proxy non-replication, not exact non-reproduction and not refutation.
Words before notation

Use labels that preserve what was and was not tested

Exact reproduction

Recompute the original result from the same data snapshot, code, environment, and protocol. We do not have those complete original materials.

Numerical replication

Independently implement the published protocol and obtain sufficiently matching values on data with demonstrated parity.

Directional replication

Recover a preregistered qualitative ordering, such as JM beating both benchmarks on Sharpe and buy-and-hold on drawdown.

Proxy replication

Retest a paper-shaped question with documented substitute data. The proxy deviations must travel with every result.

Non-replication

The preregistered replication criterion did not appear in the new experiment. It names an outcome, not automatically a cause.

Refutation

Evidence strong enough to reject a claim after plausible data and protocol explanations are resolved. The current project has not reached that level.

Provenance is the history of where code, data, and claims came from. Archive means frozen historical material retained for explanation, not supported active software. Active stack means the one package and CLI allowed to produce current evidence. A historical file can be useful provenance while being invalid as a current runner.

A verified defect is a problem demonstrated from code, mathematics, or a failed contract. An accepted approximation is a deliberate deviation whose narrower meaning is labeled. An unresolved ambiguity is a missing detail that may matter but whose effect has not been isolated. Keeping these categories separate prevents a list of uncertainties from becoming an accusation.

Attribution is a controlled experiment that changes one suspected difference while holding the rest fixed. Merely observing that v7 differs from the paper does not attribute the gap. Gate is a frozen condition that determines whether later work may open. Here, failure of the fixed-baseline directional gate blocks adaptive-lambda testing.

A negative result can be scientifically useful and technically successful. The current run completed, passed integrity checks, and returned an unfavorable scientific outcome. Engineering failure would mean missing inputs, broken execution, unverifiable metrics, or a changed rule after seeing results.

What changed after the audit

Separate useful work from claims that could not survive review

The earliest active package mixed a minute-frequency research stack with a later daily paper-inspired stack. That created two runnable interpretations of the product. Root reports and teaching pages could describe stale experiments, while ignored generated inputs were required to rerun daily scripts. The cleanup froze both histories under archive/legacy-minute/ and archive/pre-audit-daily/. Active code no longer imports either archive.

Daily P0 was an approximation, not a reproduction. Besides different data and cash, it omitted the paper's exact 3,000-day daily online decoder and monthly eight-year validation chain. Candidate penalties frequently selected the upper grid boundary, and ties favored larger smoothing. Opening performance while the optimum presses against a search boundary leaves the baseline under-specified.

P1 explored a time-varying scalar penalty but did not establish a robust improvement over fixed JM. That is a legitimate exploratory null, yet it could not answer the planned adaptive claim because the fixed baseline itself lacked paper parity and the validation surface was not fully closed. V7 therefore returned to fixed JM first.

P2 attempted different costs for entering and leaving one of two states. The external audit and archived handoff showed that the proposed zero-diagonal two-state transition objective collapsed into a symmetric recurring switch penalty plus an endpoint term. It could shift a boundary or threshold but could not encode distinct persistent durations in the claimed way. The P2 interpretation and older conclusion-bearing commit language were explicitly withdrawn.

Not everything was discarded. The legacy work contained tested data loaders, causal backtest ideas, train-only scaling, a t+2 timeline in the daily pivot, and an exact time-varying dynamic program checked against a brute-force oracle. The DP remains active in tv_jump.py, while unsupported runners and claims remain frozen. This is surgical provenance: preserve what is valid, withdraw what is not, and stop parallel pipelines.

Fair attributionThe corrective handoff identifies the earlier daily work as produced by a Claude session and records its own withdrawals. This chapter describes properties verified in archived files and mathematics. It does not infer intent, competence, or blame from a research workflow that failed later audit gates.
What v7 now establishes

A verified run can still return an unfavorable answer

The v7 source bundle uses Yahoo ^SP500TR with FRED DTB3 for the United States; Yahoo ^GDAXI with the FRED/OECD German three-month interbank series for Germany; and Yahoo ^N225 with a Bank of Japan three-month call-rate series for Japan. The US index is a total-return candidate, DAX is a performance-index candidate with documented vendor uncertainty, and Nikkei is explicitly a price index excluding dividends. German and Japanese cash are proxies rather than local Treasury bills.

The downloader preserves raw responses and canonical observations, records source metadata and hashes, rejects definition splicing, and stops at 2023. The feature pipeline applies causal availability lags to cash, preserves missingness, uses the paper-shaped excess-return features, and computes eligibility from actual coverage. No synthetic 1970 history is invented to force a 1990 start.

The fixed JM uses upstream jumpmodels.JumpModel, trailing 3,000-row scaling and fitting, semiannual centroid refits, and daily terminal online states for every penalty. The HMM fits daily on trailing 3,000 log returns with ten deterministic starts and strict convergence acceptance. Monthly selection scores eight years of already generated candidate strategy returns under the same cost and delay used OOS. Grid boundaries must pass before metrics open, and all three models share complete comparison dates within each market-delay group.

The sealed run verifier has just rechecked 131 inventory files, 18 boundary rows, and 27 metric rows from trade paths. Its maximum reconstruction difference is only floating-point CSV round-trip noise within the frozen tolerance. Those facts establish internal integrity; they do not make substitute inputs identical to the paper.

The preregistered primary gate requires fixed JM Sharpe above both HMM and buy-and-hold and a smaller maximum-drawdown magnitude than buy-and-hold in every market. V7 passed all search-boundary checks but zero markets passed all three directional conditions. Germany missed the buy-and-hold Sharpe condition; Japan missed both Sharpe comparisons and the drawdown condition; the United States missed the HMM Sharpe condition.

The frozen conclusion is therefore proxy non-replication; adaptive work remains blocked. The generated report linked from the README contains exact current metrics and source limitations. This authored chapter does not duplicate those values because regenerated verified evidence is the source of truth.

Connection to the named paper

The result disagreement is real; its cause is not identified

Shu, Yu, and Mulvey, arXiv:2402.05272v3 reports the preferred JM directional ordering across the S&P 500, DAX, and Nikkei 225 over 1990–2023. V7 does not recover that ordering across its later free-proxy samples. These two statements can both be true because the evidence objects differ.

Paper parity would require the exact Bloomberg total-return fields, GFD local bill identifiers, yield-to-daily-return conversion, data availability and missingness conventions, candidate grids, tie rules, initialization, convergence, labeling, review dates, and intermediate expected outputs. The ready-to-send request in docs/author-data-request.txt asks the authors for those items.

If original data and code reproduce the published tables, differential attribution should compare returns, features, states, monthly choices, trades, then metrics one stage at a time. If exact data remain unavailable, the exact-reproduction question remains unresolved; a transparent proxy study may continue only under its own estimand and label.

Mathematical layer

Formalize data parity, the gate, and the withdrawn asymmetry

One dividend changes every downstream object

rtprice=PtPt−1−1,rttotal=Pt+DtPt−1−1

The difference is Dt/Pt−1 before tax and reinvestment complications. That difference changes excess return, EWM features, standardized windows, JM distances, fitted states, validation Sharpe, selected smoothing, positions, and final metrics. Data parity is part of the model experiment.

The directional gate is a conjunction, not an average

gm=1{SmJM>SmHMM}1{SmJM>SmBH}1{|MDDmJM|<|MDDmBH|},G=∏m∈{US,DE,JP}gm

gmPer-market gate1 only if all three conditions pass

GOverall directional gate1 only if every market passes

A strong result in one market cannot compensate for failure in another because multiplication implements the preregistered "all three" requirement. V7 has gm=0 for each market, hence G=0. Changing the gate to an average after seeing this outcome would be post-hoc claim selection.

Why the old two-state P2 could not identify asymmetric persistence

aN01+bN10=a+b2Nswitch+a−b2(sT−s0)

Here N01 counts 0→1 transitions, N10 counts 1→0 transitions, Nswitch is their sum, and a,b are the proposed costs. In a binary path, transitions alternate, so N01−N10=sT−s0. Substituting sum and difference gives the identity.

The first term is one symmetric cost per switch. The second depends only on endpoints, not how long either state lasted. Repeated block resets turn that endpoint term into a repeated boundary effect, but still do not create separate duration hazards. A genuine persistence-asymmetry claim needs a parameterization with identifiable state-specific staying or duration behavior, then a new frozen experiment.

Evidence and boundaries

What is verified, what is inferred, and what remains unopened

Verified nowThe paper PDF identity matches the locked hash. Archived code supports the documented legacy deviations and P2 identity. The official v7 run is complete through 2023, its inventory and accounting re-verify, all boundary checks pass, and its frozen directional gate fails in all three markets. The active package, tests, config, and report generator are one consistent stack.

It is reasonable to infer that data definition and shifted sample could contribute to disagreement because those differences are large and economically relevant. It is not verified that either one is the dominant cause. The v7 outcome could also reflect unresolved implementation conventions, interactions among differences, or genuine instability of the paper's reported ordering across periods and proxies.

Do not overclaim the engineering resultHashes prove identity relative to recorded bytes, tests prove specified software behavior on covered cases, and recomputation proves internal accounting consistency. None proves that Yahoo equals Bloomberg, that a proxy cash rate equals a Treasury bill, or that a historical strategy will work in the future.

No post-2023 evidence has been opened for this course, and no adaptive experiment has been run after the fixed gate failed. The previously planned adaptive endpoint therefore has no result. Saying "adaptive failed" would be false; it was not tested under the frozen research order.

There are three defensible next scientific paths, each requiring a new task. Obtain author or licensed data and pursue numerical reproduction. Run controlled data/period attribution while preserving the proxy label. Or formally change the research question to performance on modern public proxies and preregister a fresh baseline before any extension. None authorizes changing completed v7.

If a future fixed baseline clears its gate, an adaptive model must contain fixed JM as a nested case, expose identifiable mathematics, use the same causal validation budget, and be tested on untouched evidence. A more complicated formula is not automatically a scientific contribution; it must answer a precise limitation and survive comparison against its simpler parent.

Recall: why is Japan the clearest data proxy?

The v7 Nikkei input is explicitly a price index and excludes dividends, while the paper states a Bloomberg total-return series. Its cash series is also a monthly call-rate proxy rather than the paper's local three-month Treasury bill.

Calculation: price versus total return

If price rises from 100 to 101 and pays a $2 distribution, price return is 1%. A simple gross total return is (101+2)/100−1=3%. A $100 investment differs by $2 before other conventions.

Error diagnosis: average two passing conditions

The gate is conjunctive. Passing drawdown and one Sharpe comparison does not make two-thirds of a market pass. Every specified condition must be true, and every market must pass.

Error diagnosis: call P2 state-specific persistence

For a zero-diagonal binary transition matrix, unequal entry and exit costs reduce to symmetric switching plus an endpoint term. Demonstrate an identifiable state-duration or staying mechanism before attaching a persistence interpretation.

Cumulative recall from Chapters 1–13

Money and data definitions determine returns; returns determine risk and features; features feed HMM and JM; dynamic programming creates online states; validation chooses a complete delayed strategy; artifacts preserve evidence; claim labels preserve scope. The final conclusion is only as strong as the weakest link.
